OUT! Bowled. Shaheen Afridi to Mustafizur Rahman. Yorker, to leg on the front foot defending, missed to. Shaheen Afridi has ripped through Mustafizur with another top class yorker, what a delivery and what a performance. Afridi finished with figures of 6-35 in turn producing the best ever figures by a Pakistan bowler in a World Cup, at the age of just 19. Bangladesh will be disappointed with their chase, with all apart from Shakib failing to pass 35 runs. However today belongs to Pakistan and Shaheen Afridi who end their tournament on a high. We will be saying goodbye to both these teams for this year, however both have impressed throughout.
