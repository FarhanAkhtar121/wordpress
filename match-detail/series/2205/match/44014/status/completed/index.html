SIX! Peter Siddle to Moises Henriques. Full toss, outside off stump on the front foot flick, well timed in the air under control over deep mid wicket for 6 runs. Henriques finishes it in style, ferociously whipping the ball into the stands. Sydney Sixers have comprehensively beaten Adelaide Strikers at the SCG to boost their prospects of making the semi-finals. It was a complete performance by the men in magenta, they seem to be peaking at the right time in this edition of the BBL. The Strikers total of 8-124 never looked enough as they were stifled by a parsimonious spell of 8-124 by Ben Dwarshuis. A couple of early wickets gave the visitors a glimmer of hope before Daniel Hughes and Moises Henriques put on 106 unbeaten runs to cruise to the target with a massive 35 balls remaining. The Big Bash action continues tomorrow when the Thunder travel to Docklands to take on the Renegades.
