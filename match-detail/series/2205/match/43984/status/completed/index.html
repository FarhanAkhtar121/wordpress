Sydney Sixers have sealed a thrilling five wicket victory over Brisbane Heat to start BBL cricket in 2019 in fine style. Chris Lynn's typically brutal 84 launched the hosts up to 7-164, but Moises Henriques responded with a powerful half century of his own. Jordan Silk's composed innings took Sixers to the brink of victory before it was finished with three balls to spare by Tom Curran. That means the Heat are still to register a victory this season, whereas the Sixers jump up to second in the table. While the crowd might be disappointed with the result, they have been treated to a great evening of entertainment as the match see-sawed back and forth under the sun at Metricon Stadium.
