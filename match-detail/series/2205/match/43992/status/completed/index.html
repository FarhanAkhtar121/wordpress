SIX! James Faulkner to Mohammad Nabi. Slower length ball, off stump on the front foot Slog, well timed in the air under control over deep cover for 6 runs. Nabi crashes one over the cover boundary and that will be that. Hurricanes posted a reasonable total after losing wickets in the powerplay and having to rebuild. Finch and Harper took the Rengades into a good position before Dan Christian powered them over the line with some lusty blows. Hurricanes may feel like that game was one that got away, had their tactics with ball in hand been better adjusted to the conditions they might have found themselves still unbeaten. As it is they have suffered their first defeat and that win is a valuable boost to the Renegades.
